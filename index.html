BluSkills Security & Security Chauffeur - The operators behind the service

BluSkills operators are both highly trained and experienced. They have employed their skills in UK towns and cities for executives, through to high threat environments overseas protecting high risk individuals.

Every operator is trained in Close protection, advanced driving, surveillance awareness and in life saving medical aid.

All BluSkills operators are known personally to us and are vetted and screened in accordance with BS7858:2012. They have been regularly exposed to highly confidential information and understand the need for total confidentiality.

BluSkills are holders of product certification in the delivery of Close Protection services. The bsi kitemark is a symbol of our commitment to excellence and to delivering the highest standards of service to our customers.

The Close Protection service covers both our Security Chauffeur & Close Protection services. Rest assured you're in safe hands. As part of product certification we have to demonstrate our operators are screened to exacting standards, properly trained, licensed and that we are insured.
